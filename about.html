Our Background

Who we are & What we do?

Draped is a trendy streetwear fashion brand that is dedicated to make their customers feel good about themselves. The brand message of draped is “Mixed style and confidence in clothing”. The brand is located in bocaue bulacan but they deliver clothing nationwide, Draped owner JJ Lagman said that the brand’s mission is to make millennials look good with enthusiasm, energy, and self expression by helping customers feel good about themselves while enjoying fashion.

Draped is a lifestyle brand devoted to presenting a streetwear culture fuelled by aesthetic, hip, music, and trend to youth nationwide. This support for the culture can be seen through the different products of Draped, and through the connection it creates with the community. Such products are graphic tees, jackets, and other accessories made for men and women. The business uses social media platforms to reach a wider audience as well as promote their products.
